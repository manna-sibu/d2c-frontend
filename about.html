Are We?

D2C Stories is a platform dedicated to empowering D2C brands in the digital age. Through our blog and video features, we highlight their journey, growth, and milestones, telling compelling stories that showcase their achievements. We provide brands with the opportunity to join a thriving D2C community, connecting with like-minded businesses and industry leaders while expanding their reach into new markets. Additionally, with our expert technical and marketing support, we help brands build their eCommerce sites from scratch or enhance their existing platforms. Equipped with the right tools, guidance, and strategies, we ensure brands have everything they need to succeed.

Where your brand’s journey meets limitless possibilities!

Our Team

At D2C Stories, we’re a passionate team, each bringing something unique to the table to empower D2C brands.

Trisha Mahapatra is the founder of D2C Stories, a platform that helps brands navigate the world of digital marketing and eCommerce. Trisha’s journey began as a teacher coordinator, but her fondness for advertisements—whether print or TV—soon led her to the world of digital marketing. With a Master’s in English Literature and a background in Mass Communication, she shifted from education to digital marketing, where she developed a knack for storytelling and business.

Her time at BlueHorse Software, initially as a social network marketer and later as a digital marketer, helped Trisha discover how marketing could truly benefit brands. Her growing interest in eCommerce, combined with her creativity in advertising and her teaching background, ultimately led her to launch D2C Stories... Read more

A creative powerhouse, Milan Bhowmick is the mind behind the stunning visuals at D2C Stories. With a B.Com degree and professional design training, Milan blends creativity with technical prowess. He has crafted exceptional websites and logos for numerous brands, ensuring each design is both functional and visually captivating. His expertise in modern design software and eye for detail help D2C brands stand out in a crowded digital space.

With over 20 years of experience in eCommerce and tech innovation, Vineet Agarwala is the guiding force behind D2C Stories. As the founder of

BlueHorse Software and co-founder of multiple ventures like Second Innings, iPathsala, OneTapHelp, Growtrail, and TezCommerce, Vineet brings unparalleled expertise in web, mobile app development, and digital solutions. Armed with a Bachelor’s in Information Technology and a PG Diploma in Business Management from NMIMS, he is passionate about building cutting-edge tech solutions that drive success for D2C brands.
